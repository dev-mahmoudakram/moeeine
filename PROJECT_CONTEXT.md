# Project Context — Haramain Mobility Service

## Concept

The project is a premium smart mobility service for visitors of the Two Holy Mosques. It provides electric carts, wheelchairs, and trained companions for elderly visitors, people with disabilities, and visitors who need an easier, safer, and more dignified way to move around Haram areas and nearby locations.

The website should position the service as:

> A luxury Saudi hospitality and smart mobility platform designed to serve Guests of Allah with dignity, comfort, privacy, and operational excellence.

## Service Scope

The service may include:

- Electric cart rental
- Wheelchair rental
- Trained companion service
- Mobility support for elderly visitors
- Mobility support for people with disabilities
- Hotel pickup and delivery
- Bus station support
- Parking area support
- Central area pickup/drop-off
- Outside central area pickup/drop-off
- Public location support
- Service points
- Pre-booking
- Online payment
- Operations center
- Customer support
- Technical support
- Field supervisors
- Logistics team
- GPS fleet tracking
- Battery monitoring
- Maintenance and charging
- Reports and dashboards

## Target Audiences

1. Pilgrims and visitors who need mobility support.
2. Families booking for elderly parents or relatives.
3. Visitors with disabilities or limited mobility.
4. Hotels and hospitality providers.
5. Operators, service partners, and investors.
6. Official or semi-official stakeholders reviewing the project.

## Core Message

Do not describe the service as a normal cart rental business.

Use this positioning:

> We do not only provide a cart. We provide a dignified, safe, and organized mobility experience for the Guests of Allah.

## Brand Feeling

The website should feel:

- Premium
- Luxury
- Calm
- Respectful
- Spiritual
- Saudi-inspired
- Operationally mature
- Human-centered
- Trustworthy

## Design Inspiration

Use visual cues inspired by:

- Haram architecture
- Islamic arches
- Subtle geometric patterns
- Dark emerald and deep teal atmospheres
- Luxury gold accents
- Warm ivory text
- Soft turquoise highlights
- High-end hospitality websites
- Premium product showcase pages

## Important Content Rules

- Arabic is the default language.
- English is secondary.
- Arabic tone must be formal, elegant, and human.
- Do not claim official partnership unless explicitly confirmed.
- Mention readiness for integration, not official affiliation.
- Use mobile app screenshots as preview only, not as a redesign target.
